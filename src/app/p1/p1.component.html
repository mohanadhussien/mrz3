Kiosks with Transformers

Kiosks are manufactured by MRZ.The kiosk comprise of an enclosure containing medium voltage compartment, distribution transformer compartment and low voltage compartment .The enclosure is made of sheet steel, painted with a light grey electrostatic powder painting, the finish coat of the kiosk is grey color, The roof of the kiosk is slope to prevent accumulation of water rain , The base of the kiosk is provided with steel structure for lifting or hanging. each compartment have its door and each door is provided with suitable gaskets and locks with master key. 1- Medium voltage compartment:- It is designed to accommodate different types Ring main units : SF6 type & Air insulated type with rated voltage 12kv or 24kv according to customer requirements .it is designed according to IEC 62271-202. 2- Transformer compartment:- It is designed to accommodate different types and ratings of transformers and it is designed to give enough natural cooling. it is designed according to IEC 60076. 3- Low voltage compartment:- It is designed to accommodate different types of switching devices such as MCCB'S ,ACB'S,Fuse
